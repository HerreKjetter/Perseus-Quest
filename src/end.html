Your Quest Ends

You claim Andromeda in marriage as your reward. However, she was betrothed to another man named Phineus. At the wedding a quarrel breaks out between you and Phineus. You turn him to stone by showing him the head of Medusa. You return to Seriphos and kill Polydectes with Medusa's head. Dictys becomes king of Seriphos. You return the gifts the gods have given you for your quest. You return to Argos and Andromeda gives birth to a son named Perses. Acrisius heads to Larissa after being exiled by his brother Proetus. You kill Proetus with Medusa's head and head to Larissa where athletic games are being held. Acrisius attends and during a game of quoit you accidently kill him. Fulfilling the prophecy. Megapenthes, son of Proetus, ascends the throne and becomes King of Argos.

You on the other hand, give the head of Medusa as a gift to Athena and found the city of Mycenae where you become king. You and Andromeda live a long life and oversee a successful reign.
